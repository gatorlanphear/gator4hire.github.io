    Introduction



    Welcome to Gator's Learning Program.

    The trainings are designed with the adult and youth learners in mind.

    Beginning courses (101) introduce programs or skills. Learners gain valuable overview knowledge and discover the basic concepts and workings of the programs.

    Intermediate courses (200) strengthen newly acquired skills from the beginning courses or act as refresher courses for those who have previously worked with certain programs.

    Advanced courses (300) will take a specific project need, or skillset requested, and develop desired outcomes.

      Rates begin at $99 per hour. Costs can be discussed for group rates and/or more advanced courses.

    Module 1: Vision Zero Tacoma

    This module includes an introduction to Vision Zero Tacoma and provide national and local statistics of vehicle and pedestrian fatalities and severe crashes.

    It will act as the foundation and introduction to the learning in the subsequent modules.

    This will include a knowledge check that is based upon the recalling statistical information provided.

    BEGIN

    Module 2: Driver Safety Best Practices

    This module will review and reinforce driver safety best practices based on guidelines created by state and local driving rules and regulations.

    You can evaluate and reinforce best practices and examine your own driving experience and skills.

    You will focus on concepts of distracted driving, road awareness, and ways to make sure you are safe even before starting the vehicle.

    This module includes an online accessible or printable checklist.
